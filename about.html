The Real Problem in Kigali

Residents in Gasabo, Kicukiro, and Nyarugenge districts face irregular waste collection, illegal dumping, and limited awareness of green initiatives. EcoKigali connects citizens directly with local authorities using realistic 2026 data.

  • 🌍 42% of households report delayed waste collection (simulated data)
  • 🗑️ 187 illegal dumping hotspots identified last quarter
  • 🤝 Scalable to every district and future cities in Rwanda

Explore Kigali Green Initiatives

Real ongoing projects making Kigali one of Africa’s cleanest and greenest cities

🌳 Green City Kigali

600-hectare sustainable city on Kinyinya Hill. Aims for carbon neutrality by 2050 with eco-park, green buildings and 30,000 affordable homes.

Major Project
🤝 Umuganda

Monthly nationwide community clean-up day. Every last Saturday, citizens clean streets, plant trees and remove illegal dumps.

Community Program
🌿 Wetland Restoration

Nyandungu Eco-Park and other wetlands restoration. Part of SUNCASA project planting 3 million indigenous trees by 2026.

Nature-Based Solution
🚫 Plastic Ban & Waste Management

Strict single-use plastic ban since 2008 + door-to-door collection and recycling programs across all districts.

📍 Kigali Master Plan 2050

"Kigali Yacu" – Focus on green growth, public parks, green transport and climate resilience for all 3 districts.
